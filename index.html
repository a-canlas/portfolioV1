About Me

Creating things and serving others has always been my motivator in life. I have a B.S. in Recreation and over 15 years of experience in the hospitality sector.

My love of technology stems from childhood where I tried to make programs for other kids to enjoy. My passion would re-ignite when I discovered programming and in 2019, I ended up taking the plunge by going out to Orange County to study Web Development at LearningFuze.

While at LearningFuze, I gained many new skill sets such as working with React.js, jQuery, Express.js, PostgreSQL, AWS, NPM, Node.js and most importantly with others in a team setting. I completed over 800 hours of work time within a 12 week period. The program challenged me and helped me grow as a person. I would do it all again in a heartbeat!

With the knowledge that I gained from being at LearningFuze along with my interpersonal skills from working with a wide range of people in hospitality, I feel that I can be an invaluable member of your team.

When not in front of my trusty MacBook Pro, you can find me out hiking on the trails, trying all sorts of different foods, or drawing silly cartoon animals.
